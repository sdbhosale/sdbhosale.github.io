I am a graduate student at the Robotics Institute in Masters in Robotic Systems Development program. I am greatly inspired by machine vision and it's applications. I have done several projects in 3D vision and image understaning using classical and deep learning approaches. My goal is leverage these skills to make autonomous systems better.

Research

Most of my projects are on inferring the physical world (shape, motion, color, light, etc) from images. Following is a sample of my work in this area.

Single view to 3D- Classical CV [Website]
Aug 2022 - Dec 2022
  • Estimated camera using annotations and image to 3D correspondences. Followed it by generating a 3D point cloud from a single image with known annotations.
Single view to 3D- Lerning Based [Website]
Jan 23
  • Explored various loss functions to optimize for 3D represenations such as point clouds, mesh and voxels.
  • Trained a decoder network to generate 3D from single-views. Investigated the effect of hyperparameters to interpret the models and quality of meshes.
Structure from Motion [Website]
Aug 2022 - Dec 2022
  • Implemented methods to calculate Fundamental Matrix form image correspondences
  • Developed algorithms to calculate Essential matrix using 7-point and 8-point methods with known camera intrinsics
  • Used bundle adjustment to reconstruct 3D and get cameras for multiple views
  • Implemented entire incremental SfM pipeline to generate and optimize a 3D reconstruction of the scene and its the cameras. Triangulated 3D by computing Fundamental and Essential matrix from image correspondences by use of RANSAC.
Object Tracking and Motion Detection
Aug 2021 - Dec 2021
  • Implemented the Lucas-Kanade optical-flow method to track objects in video sequences, and extended the method to detect and track the motion of multiple objects in a scene.
Image Classification Projects
Aug 2021 - May 2022
  • Built a scene-recognition system based on the visual bag-of-words(Spatial Pyramid Matching) approach to classify images into different scenes.
  • Developed a classification system by tuning a pre-existing ResNet model to perform multi-class and multi-label classification in PyTorch on Pascal-VOC dataset.
Shape Estimation Using Photometric Stereo
Jan 23
  • Developed an implementation of both calibrated and uncalibrated photometric stereo to estimate the shape of an object and scene lighting. Analyzed the potential ambiguity in the estimated shape introduced by the joint estimation process.
Projective Geometry and Homography [Website]
Aug 2022 - Dec 2022
  • Performed affine and metric rectification of the images taken through the perspective cameras assume=ing pinhole camera model using manual annotations
  • Estimated homographies between two views using point correspondences
Scene Rendering with PyTorch3D [Website]
Jan 2023
  • Studied the 3D represenations and conversion between represenations such as mesh, point cloud, parametric & implicit surfaces conversion between them.
  • Explored camera transformation and rendering methods in PyTorch3D.
Weakly Supervised Object Localization
Jan 2022 - May 2022
  • Trained object detectors using only image -level annotations by a modifying AlexNet. The network reasoned about locations of the object in the image without bounding-box annotations.
